# Paper Review: MM-KWS (INTERSPEECH 2024)

**Multi-modal Prompts for Multilingual User-defined KWS**

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Authors** | Zhiqi Ai, Zhiyong Chen, Shugong Xu |
| **Method** | Multi-modal prompts (phonetic + text embeddings) |
| **Results** | 90.3% AUC on unseen multilingual keywords across 5 language families |
| **Relevance** | High — multilingual approach relevant for Vietnamese. Phonetic prompts outperform text-only by 11% on tonal languages. |

**Key Takeaway:** Phonetic prompts significantly outperform text-only for tonal languages (like Vietnamese). Validates phoneme-level approach for Vietnamese UDKWS.
